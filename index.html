How to play the Pic-Game

  • The Player 1 starts the game by rolling the dice, if he gets a "1", the dice is switched to the other player and vice-versa.


  • If the player rolls the dice and didn't get a "1", he can either continue rolling or click on "Hold" button to save the current score in the "Total Score".


  • If the player rolls a "1", the current score becomes "0", and the dice is switched to the other player.


  • The player wins the game if he reaches first to a score equal or superior than 50.
